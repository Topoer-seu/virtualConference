ICML 2025 - Call for Papers

The 42nd International Conference on Machine Learning

July 20-25, 2025 | Nanjing, China

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: March 1, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: May 15, 2025
  • Camera-ready Submission: June 10, 2025
  • Conference Dates: July 20-25, 2025

About ICML 2025

The International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML) is the premier gathering of professionals dedicated to the advancement of machine learning. ICML 2025 will be hosted in the vibrant city of Nanjing, China, offering a perfect blend of historical charm and modern innovation.

Call for Papers

ICML 2025 invites high-quality submissions of original research in all areas of machine learning. Submissions should present innovative ideas, rigorous methods, and significant contributions to the field.

Topics of Interest

  • Supervised, Unsupervised, and Semi-supervised Learning
  • Deep Learning and Neural Networks
  • Reinforcement Learning and Decision Making
  • Probabilistic Models and Inference
  • Optimization Methods in Machine Learning
  • Applications of Machine Learning in Real-world Systems
  • Fairness, Accountability, and Ethics in AI
  • Explainability and Interpretability
  • Efficient and Scalable Machine Learning
  • ML for Climate Change and Sustainability

Submission Guidelines

  • All submissions must be original and not under review elsewhere.
  • Papers should be formatted using the ICML 2025 LaTeX template.
  • The maximum paper length is 8 pages, excluding references and supplementary materials.
  • All papers will undergo double-blind peer review.
  • Submissions must be made through the official ICML 2025 submission portal: icml2025.org/submissions

Venue

The conference will be held at the Nanjing International Exhibition Center, located in the heart of the city. Nanjing is renowned for its rich history, cultural heritage, and vibrant academic community.
